Biography

Miss Qian Feng is currently a PhD Candidate (supported by China Scholarship Council: CSC) in School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Melbourne, Australia, working with Dr Yao-ban Chan and Dr Heejung Shim. Prior to this, she received her Master Degree in epidemiology and medical statistics at Renmin University of China(RUC) at July 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Danhui Yi. Besides, she also cooperated with Assoc. Prof. Yang Li. She earned her Bachelor degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from China Agricultural University(CAU) in 2014, where she worked with Prof. Yuqin Yao and Dr Yanfei Wang. Her CV is here.

She is interested in Fuzzy Mathematics, Variable Selection, Data Mining, Machine Learning and Bioinformatics. She is currently doing some researches on Phylogenetic Networks about VAR genes in parasites related with malaria.
