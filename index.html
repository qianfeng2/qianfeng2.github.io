Biography

Hansheng Xue is currently a research assistant (supported by Assis. Prof. Junyi Li) in School of Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, China. Prior to this, he received his Master Degree in computer science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT), under the supervision of Prof. Yadong Wang. Besides, He also cooperated with Assoc. Prof. Jiajie Peng (Northwestern Polytechnical University, China) and Assoc. Prof. Jin Chen (University of Kentucky, USA). He earned his Bachelor degree from Nanjing University of Technology (NJUT) in 2015, where he worked with Prof. Yu Pan. His CV is here.

He is interested in Bioinformatics, Data Mining and Deep Learning. He is currently doing some researches on Graph Clustering with applications in multiple biological networks.
